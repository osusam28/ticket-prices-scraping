About Cincinnati Bengals

Team Name: Cincinnati Bengals

Division: AFC North

Year Founded: 1968

Stadium Name: Paul Brown Stadium

Who dey, who dey, who dey think gonna beat dem Bengals? Nobody!

If you’re going to take in a game at Paul Brown Stadium, be ready to chant! Bengals fans are loud and proud, and will always be ready to cheer their team to victory. The Bengals started out in 1968 as a part of the AFL, and when the AFL-NFL merger happened two years later, they became a part of the AFC Conference. Cincinnati got off to a strong start in 1970, and by the end of that season, they had won the AFC Central division title. Over time, the Bengals have picked up quite a few playoff appearances and Division titles, and they deliver consistently exciting games in Paul Brown Stadium.
